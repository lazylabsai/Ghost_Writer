Non-Tech Onboarding Guide

You don't need to be a programmer to use Ghost Writer. Follow these three simple steps to have the ultimate AI assistant ready for your next big interview.

1

Installation & Setup

Windows Users: Download the .exe file from the home page. Double-click it to install. If Windows shows a "Windows protected your PC" popup, click "More info" and then "Run anyway" (this just means we are a new app).

Mac Users: Download the .dmg file. Open it, and drag the Ghost Writer icon into your Applications folder.

When you open the app for the first time, you will see a Settings Menu. You just need to paste in an "API Key" to give the AI its brain.

Recommended Free API Tiers

  • Google Gemini: Highly capable intelligence with a generous free tier. Get a free Gemini API Key here.
  • Groq: Lightning-fast open source models with a free tier. Get a free Groq API Key here.
  • Fast Free Transcription (STT): In the Audio settings, you can select "Groq (Whisper)" and use the exact same Groq API key you got above for completely free, instant speech-to-text!

Just copy the long string of text from those websites, paste it into the Ghost Writer settings box, and hit Save!

2

Uploading Your Resume

Ghost Writer is incredibly smart, but it needs to know who you are to give good answers!

Click the Settings icon in the app, and find the "Context / Resume" section.

  • Copy and paste the text from your Resume into the box.
  • If you are interviewing for a specific job, paste the Job Description in the job description box.

Now, when the interviewer asks, "Tell me about a time you showed leadership," the AI will look at your actual past jobs and write an answer specific to your life!

3

During the Interview

Before jumping on your Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams call, simply click Start Meeting inside Ghost Writer.

How it listons

Ghost Writer automatically listens to the speakers coming through your headphones and your own microphone. It types out the transcript in real-time. You don't need to change any Zoom settings—it just works.

Automatic Answers

As soon as the interviewer finishes asking a question, a glowing box will appear on your screen with a perfectly crafted answer in bullet points. Just read it naturally!

The Magic Screenshot Button

If the interviewer shares their screen to show you a chart, a block of code, or a presentation, press Ctrl+H on your keyboard. Ghost Writer will silently take a screenshot of your screen, read the image, and tell you what to say about it!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can the interviewer see Ghost Writer on my screen?

No. Ghost Writer uses special "stealth" windows. If you share your screen on Zoom, Teams, or Meet, Ghost Writer becomes completely invisible to the other person. Only you can see it.

It's not hearing the audio. What do I do?

Make sure you aren't wearing Bluetooth headphones that are rapidly switching between devices. If audio fails, go to the Settings gear, find the Audio tab, and change your microphone/speaker to specifically match the ones you are using.

Is it safe to type my Resume in?

Yes. Everything is saved locally on your own computer. Ghost Writer does not upload your resume to a central database.
