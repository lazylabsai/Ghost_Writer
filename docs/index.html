Full Action Guide

1. Stealth Initialization

Launch Ghost Writer and immediately press Ctrl+B to hide the overlay. Use the PWA Sync on your phone to keep your primary screen 100% clean during high-stakes calls.

2. Context Grounding

Drag your architectural diagrams, project requirements, or technical docs into the grounding zone (Settings -> Grounding) to ensure AI responses are mathematically aligned with your current task.

3. Real-Time Logic Injection

When a complex question arises, hit Ctrl+J. This triggers our reasoning chain (Gemini 1.5 Flash/Pro) to analyze the latest screenshots and Whisper-transcribed audio simultaneously.
